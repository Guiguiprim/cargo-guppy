//!
//! * [indicatif](https://docs.rs/indicatif)
//! * [console](https://docs.rs/console)
//!
//! # Crate Contents

//! * Confirmation prompts
//! * Input prompts (regular and password)
//! * Input validation
//! * Selections prompts (single and multi)
//! * Other kind of prompts
//! * Editor launching
